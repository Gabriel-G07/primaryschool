Resources & Facilities

ABC Secondary School offers a host of opportunities for learning and a vast range of resources to assist students with their studies. Well-equipped class rooms and laboratories, wi-fi internet access on campus foster a safe and inclusive environment which inspires a lifelong love of learning. The college invites eminent achievers from different walks of life to the campus to share their insights that help to augment the vision of students.

Hygienic Cafeteria

Cafeteria provides hygienic meals to students.

Sports

To unearth the hidden aptitude of students the college has special provision of sports. The Sports Department provides the necessary sports facilities.

Library

The college has a library to enhance effective and holistic teaching/learning process. Along with course books, reference books help supplement text-book teaching-learning.

Computer Lab

The College has a well equipped computer lab having unlimited internet facility.

Transportation

The college provides transportation facility for easy conveyance of students in and around the Ring Road.
